About BritLister Pro

BritLister Pro is a comprehensive cross-listing and inventory management platform designed specifically for online sellers who operate across multiple marketplaces. Our platform helps sellers save time, reduce errors, and grow their business by automating the tedious aspects of multi-platform selling.

Our Mission

We empower online sellers to focus on what matters most - sourcing great products and growing their business - while we handle the technical complexity of managing listings across multiple platforms.

Etsy Integration

Our Etsy integration helps sellers:

  • Automatically sync inventory between Etsy and other platforms to prevent overselling
  • Bulk create and update Etsy listings with AI-generated content optimized for Etsy's search
  • Manage pricing across platforms while respecting Etsy's handmade and vintage requirements
  • Process and optimize product images to meet Etsy's quality standards
  • Monitor listing performance and make data-driven optimization decisions

Data Access: We use read and write access to listing data, inventory levels, and product images. We do not access customer personal information, payment data, or private messages. All data is handled in compliance with Etsy's API Terms of Service and GDPR requirements.

Privacy & Security: We use OAuth 2.0 for secure authentication and only request the minimum necessary permissions. User data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

How It Works

  1. Connect Your Accounts: Securely connect your eBay, Etsy, TikTok Shop, and OnBuy seller accounts using OAuth authentication.
  2. Import or Create Listings: Import existing listings from any connected platform or create new ones from scratch.
  3. Customize & Optimize: Use our AI tools to generate optimized content, process images, and customize listings for each platform.
  4. Publish Everywhere: Push your listings to all selected platforms with a single click.
  5. Automated Sync: Sit back while BritLister Pro automatically keeps inventory, prices, and listing details in sync across all platforms.
